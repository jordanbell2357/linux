> **‘start’**
> 
> Restart the output after stopping it.

> **‘lnext’**
> 
> Enter the next character typed literally, even if it is a special character. Non-POSIX.

start = <kbd>^Q</kbd> = CTRL+Q

lnext = <kbd>^V</kbd> = CTRL+V
